Key of the Spire

Sharp Locke has entered the private safe of Avidity where a particular item, the Key of the Spire, is locked away.

In order to obtain the Key you must hack the five (5) locks in place.

START

Instructions

Match the blocks in all three pillars to unlock a chamber. Unlock all three chambers to to crack the lock.

Be careful with rolling over each block as it increases the Suspicion Level of the system. The Suspicion Level will rise each time a block is engaged. If the Suspicion Level reaches the top, or you run out of time, you will be caught by Avidity authorities.
